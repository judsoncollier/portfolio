Judson is a young designer born and raised in Jacksonville, Florida. He specializes in web design, but hopes to expand into different creative outlets in 2013. He has a particular fondness for non-profits.

Recently, Judson took a sabbatical from university to design with celebrity photographer and humanitarian Jeremy Cowart in Nashville, TN. He'll be working with Jeremy on everything from his website to Help-Portrait. You can see the website he designed for Jeremy that got him the internship here.

Judson is currently accepting lots of part time work for the month of January 2013. You can ask him about it here.
